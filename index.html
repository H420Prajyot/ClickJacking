What does this mean?

Vulnerable: The target page loaded inside the iframe. This means it lacks X-Frame-Options (DENY or SAMEORIGIN) and a proper Content-Security-Policy: frame-ancestors header. An attacker could overlay decoy UI on top and trick users into clicking buttons on the hidden target page.

Protected: The target page refused to load in the iframe (shows blank or error). The site has clickjacking protections in place.
